Beyond The Major

About

Client:

Kelly Myers for Boise State University

Team:

  • - Mike Wilson
  • - AJ Marriage
  • - Tim Hammes

Dates:

April 2018 - July 2018

Description:

Kelly Myers on behalf of Boise State University approached the GIMM department to help create a brand image, a web presence, and various other media for the new campus initiative, Beyond the Major. In our first meeting with Kelly we generated plenty of excitement and new ideas for what the brand would be. By brainstorming with Kelly and thinking of design and media ideas on the fly, I was able to gain her trust on the project and scale the project upwards to more than she originally had in mind.

Deliverables:

  • Website in BSU Wordpress
  • Logo Design and Animation
  • Promotional Video

Process

Tools:

Design

From the client:

Kelly had a brand image in mind that involved three columns (descriptors) of Beyond The Major, BTM. The three words: Purpose, Opportunity, and Narrative all represented different aspects of BTM. She wanted the three parts to be represented in all the branding and design.

First Thoughts:

Coming into this project I wanted to stress a feeling of "definitions" students defining themselves through this process. As well there was a distinct focus on "being" more through this program. With this in mind I created some preliminary designs for the client.

Logo Design:

The logo was the most important peice to get right in regards to conveying the brand's message. Through the use of three chevrons, resembling the greater than symbol, we were able to achieve the clients goal.

Stage 1

The team and I spent a half day discussing and brainstorming how to visualize the three words the client had given us to describe the brand. Through multiple rounds of internal revision and some client feedback we presented her with these logo ideas. They were simple yet captured the essence of "BEing" more.

A quick sketch presented to the client

Stage 2

After recieving feedback from the client about the presented ideas we developed more of the Beyond that included the greater than symbol. Using the angle formed by the Boise State "B" we created this design to be used with the word "Beyond" and stand alone.

A chevron logo with each color representing a different BTM term

Stage 3

At this point we decided to take the three words and create three icons to represent them. These icons would be used in the animation of the logo.

The three icons representing: Purpose, Opportunity, and Narrative

For the Web:

For the web work I had to design within the brand standards of Boise State while giving this site a new energy that differentiated it from the rest of the BSU Wordpress. The client wanted a easy-to-use site so that students would be happy to interact with it and use the resources from it.

Stage 1

I focused on making the site more modular than the other Boise State pages. Using the three words describing the brand as sections of content I was able to better display the content without blocks of text.

A site design made for the clients reference

Stage 2

In the second round of revision I made a prototype site in Adobe XD and had it match the layout of the Boise State Wordpress. The clients approval of this prototype allowed me to enter into the development phase of the project.

A quick sketch presented to the client

Development

In the Code:

The development for this project was done in Boise State Wordpress; meaning I had to develop the pages using those tools. While my tools may have limited me I focused on creating an accesable and easy to use site so that students would be willing to spend time on the site. I achieved this through proper usage of tags and aria labels throughout the site, as well as testing using auditing software and screen readers.

Delivered

In Conclusion:

This project was great to work on - I had a lot of opportunities to give insight and design ideas for the BTM brand. I was happy to work on logo design, web design, and accesible development in one project.
